Who can log in?

Approved partners with credentials provisioned by Partner Ops (or SSO when enabled).

What happens after login?

You’ll be redirected to your partner dashboard where AI services and tools are available.

Can we connect this to SSO later?

Yes — this front-end is designed to easily swap local login logic for SSO/IAM validation.
